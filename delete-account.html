Delete Your Account

App: FluidCast: Podcast Player

You can request deletion of your FluidCast account and all associated data at any time using either of the methods below. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

In-app deletions are processed within 30 days. Email requests are processed immediately upon review and you will receive a confirmation.

Option 1 — Delete from within the App

  1. Open FluidCast: Podcast Player on your device.
  2. Tap the Account tab (or navigate to Settings › Account).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your deletion request will be queued and your account and all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

  1. Send an email to support@fluidcastapp.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your FluidCast account in the message body so we can locate your account.

We will process your request and permanently delete your account immediately upon review. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

Data Storage Action Retention after deletion
Email address & authentication credentials Supabase Auth Deleted None
Podcast subscriptions Supabase Database Deleted None
Episode playback progress Supabase Database Deleted None
App preferences & settings Supabase Database Deleted None
Crash & diagnostic logs Firebase Crashlytics Anonymised / dissociated from your account Up to 90 days per Firebase's standard retention policy
Aggregate analytics events Firebase Analytics Anonymised / dissociated from your account Up to 14 months per Firebase's standard retention policy
Subscription purchase records RevenueCat Dissociated from your account Retained as required by applicable law and app store policies

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, please contact us at support@fluidcastapp.com.
