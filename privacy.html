Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 October 2025

FluidCast is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app and website.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and password for authentication purposes.
  • Podcast Data: Your subscriptions, playback position, and preferences are stored securely and may be synced across devices if you use premium features.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic analytics to improve app performance and reliability. No behavioral tracking is performed.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type and OS version to ensure compatibility and support.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve FluidCast services
  • To sync your podcast data across devices (premium feature)
  • To communicate important updates or support information
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide core app functionality (such as authentication and sync).

Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard security measures. We recommend using a strong password and keeping your app updated.

Your Choices

  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time from within the app.
  • Contact support for data removal requests or questions.

Children's Privacy

FluidCast is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fluidcast.app.
